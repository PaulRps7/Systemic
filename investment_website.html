Systematic Long/Short Equity

We deploy capital through rigorous fundamental research in public equities, seeking asymmetric opportunities in global markets.

Strategy

Philosophy

Markets are inefficient at the margins. Our approach combines systematic fundamental analysis with detailed modelling to identify mispricings that emerge from behavioral biases, structural constraints, and information asymmetries.

We believe that sustainable alpha generation requires a probabilistic mindset, a unique way of thinking, and the intellectual honesty to continuously challenge our assumptions.

Our Edge

Our competitive advantage stems from three pillars: thorough financial modelling, a disciplined framework that removes emotional decision-making, and a time horizon that allows us to capitalize on market inefficiencies others cannot access.

We focus on opportunities where we possess a genuine informational or analytical advantage, declining to participate where we lack conviction or edge.

Time Horizon

We operate with a medium to long-term investment horizon, typically holding positions from several months to multiple years. This duration aligns with the time required for fundamental catalysts to materialize and for market sentiment to converge with intrinsic value.

Risk Management

Risk management is embedded throughout our process, not applied as an afterthought. We employ position sizing based on conviction and volatility, maintain strict stop-loss disciplines, and continuously stress-test our portfolio against adverse scenarios.

We view preservation of capital as paramount. The ability to participate in future opportunities depends entirely on surviving adverse environments.

Performance & Approach

Evaluating Performance

Investment performance should be evaluated across complete market cycles, not quarterly periods. We focus on risk-adjusted returns, consistency of process, and adherence to our stated strategy rather than short-term relative performance.

The most important metrics are not annual returns, but Sharpe ratios, maximum drawdown management, and the ability to compound capital over extended periods with minimal permanent impairment.

Process Over Outcome

Superior processes produce superior outcomes over time. We evaluate decisions based on the quality of analysis and reasoning at the time they were made, not solely on results.

Long-Term Thinking

Our success is measured in decades, not quarters. This perspective allows us to maintain discipline during periods of underperformance and capitalize on opportunities others cannot access.

Continuous Improvement

Markets evolve, and so must we. We maintain detailed performance attribution, conduct regular post-mortems, and invest continuously in research and infrastructure.

About

Systemic was founded on the principle that superior investment returns require differentiated thinking, rigorous analysis, and looking where most people don't.

Our team combines deep expertise in quantitative finance, fundamental research, and behavioral psychology. We are students of markets, continuously studying historical patterns, emerging trends, and the structural forces that shape asset prices.

We believe that intellectual curiosity, combined with systematic execution, creates sustainable competitive advantages in capital markets. Our research spans asset classes, geographies, and time horizons, always seeking to understand not just what is happening, but why.

This is not a business of predictions, but of probabilities. We construct portfolios designed to perform well across a range of future scenarios while maintaining strict downside protection. Our goal is to compound capital efficiently over time while preserving optionality and remaining intellectually honest about the limits of our knowledge.
